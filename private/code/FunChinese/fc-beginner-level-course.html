Beginner Level

This class is for beginner level young learns. To hear and to reproduce the critical sounds and tonal differences in Mandarin. Throughout the course, young learners will naturally acquire Mandarin through songs & rhymes, stories, poem, etc. Students participate in interactive reading activities and fun games by utilizing Chinese with the culture practices in a natural setting. we will also be introducing basic Chinese characters recognition.
